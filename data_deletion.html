Data Deletion Instructions

According to Facebook Platform rules, we must provide a Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL.

If you want to delete your activities for SK LOGIC CRM, you can remove your information by following these steps:

How to Request Data Deletion:

1. Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy" menu.

2. Click on "Settings" and then look for "Apps and Websites".

3. Find SK LOGIC CRM and click on it.

4. Click the "Remove" button.

5. Alternatively, you can send an email to support@sklogic.com with your registered phone number or email address to request permanent data deletion from our servers.

Once requested, we will process your data deletion within 7 business days.
